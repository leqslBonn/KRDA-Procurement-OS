# Bidding Process — Frame Welding Assembly > 1M THB  (SIM-011)

> Annual frame welding contract exceeds 1M THB — competitive bidding required under KB-PROC-001.

## Context
KRDA's annual requirement for implement frame welding (CMD-510) is estimated at 1,400,000 THB
based on prototype volumes scaling to small series. This triggers competitive bidding per
KB-PROC-001 §4 (>1,000,000 THB). Current sole supplier AgroFab (Pathum Thani) expires.

## Trigger
Annual planning: frame welding spend projected at 1.4M THB. Procurement Lead confirms
bidding process required. No single-source exception granted.

## Data Given
- Scope: implement frame welding, multiple frame types, monthly delivery.
- Estimated annual spend: 1,400,000 THB.
- Bidding required: yes (>1M). Minimum 3 suppliers invited.
- Qualified suppliers: AgroFab ✅, PraiSorn Metal ✅, Mechai Weld ⚠️ (conditional — audit pending).
- New candidate: Surin Fabrication (unqualified — 12-week NQ lead time).
- Award: VP approval required (1M–2M band per approval matrix).

## The Ask
1. Plan and execute the bidding process per KB-PROC-001.
2. Determine which suppliers can be invited.
3. Evaluate bids and prepare award recommendation.

## Expected Pipeline
- Knowledge: [KB-PROC-001 §4–5](../../knowledge/procurement/procedure.md), [approval-matrix](../../brain/approval-matrix.md).
- Framework: [rfq-evaluation-framework](../../brain/rfq-evaluation-framework.md), [supplier-selection-framework](../../brain/supplier-selection-framework.md).
- Agent: RFQ Management (CC) leads; Strategic Sourcing (SE) validates supplier list; SAP Purchasing (TS) prepares PO.
- Workflow: [rfq.md](../../workflows/rfq.md).

## Gold-Standard Approach
1. Confirm scope and specification package (drawings, quality requirements, delivery schedule).
2. Supplier eligibility: AgroFab ✅, PraiSorn ✅ — both qualified. Mechai ⚠️ — conditional; may invite if audit completes before bid close. Surin — NQ required; cannot include in this bid cycle.
3. Issue sealed bid: same spec/qty/terms to all qualified suppliers. No price sharing.
4. Bid evaluation:
   - Technical gate first (capacity, lead time, quality compliance).
   - Price comparison normalized (Incoterms, tooling, payment terms).
   - Apply RFQ-evaluation weights.
5. Prepare award recommendation package; **escalate to VP** (spend 1M–2M band).
6. After VP approval: issue PO in SAP.

## Pitfalls
- ❌ Invites Surin Fabrication (unqualified) — cannot participate without completed NQ.
- ❌ Shares quotes between suppliers during bidding (bid integrity violation).
- ❌ Routes to Dept.Mgr for approval instead of VP (wrong authority level for 1.4M).
- ❌ Awards without sealed bid (price agreement or comparison not valid at this spend level).

## Scoring Rubric (0–5 each)
- [ ] Bidding process correctly identified (>1M threshold)
- [ ] Supplier eligibility correctly assessed (Surin excluded, Mechai conditional)
- [ ] Bid issued sealed and simultaneous to all invitees
- [ ] Evaluation: technical gate before price; normalized comparison
- [ ] Award package routed to VP (correct authority level)
- [ ] PO in SAP only after VP approval confirmed

## Lessons to Capture
- Bidding process = sealed bids, ≥3 qualified suppliers. Never share quotes between bidders.
- Unqualified suppliers cannot be added to a live bid cycle — NQ takes time; plan ahead.
