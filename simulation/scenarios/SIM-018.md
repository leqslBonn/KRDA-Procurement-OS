# Poor quality — weld cracking in the field  (SIM-018)

> A welded sub-frame cracks in service; classify, contain, and drive corrective action.

## Context
Field reports of weld cracking on a harvester sub-frame from supplier (qualified).
Potential safety/function impact.

## Data Given
- 3 field failures; parts in stock + in production. Weld joint suspected.
- Supplier MIG weld; no recent process change reported.

## The Ask
Classify severity, contain, and set corrective action.

## Expected Pipeline
- Framework: [quality-escalation-framework](../../brain/quality-escalation-framework.md).
- Workflow: [supplier_audit](../../workflows/supplier_audit.md) (process re-audit) + CAPA.

## Gold-Standard Approach
Classify as **critical** (function/safety) → **immediate containment**: quarantine stock,
hold WIP, notify Production. **Escalate to Human Owner (L3)**. Disposition with engineering
(no use-as-is without sign-off). Root-cause (weld parameters, joint design, material) →
CAPA → supplier process re-audit; update scorecard/risk register.

## Pitfalls
- ❌ Under-classifying a safety issue.
- ❌ Continuing to ship/use without disposition.
- ❌ Closing without root cause.

## Scoring Rubric (0–5 each)
- [ ] Classified critical; contained immediately
- [ ] Escalated to Human Owner
- [ ] Disposition with engineering sign-off
- [ ] Root cause + CAPA
- [ ] Scorecard/risk updated
- [ ] Lesson captured

## Lessons to Capture
- Weld-defect root-cause; containment discipline for safety issues.
