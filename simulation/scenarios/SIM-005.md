# New supplier onboarding & qualification  (SIM-005)

> A promising new supplier appears for a strategic commodity; onboard and qualify.

## Context
New casting supplier H surfaces with attractive pricing for a strategic casting
category currently single-sourced.

## Trigger
Strategic Sourcing proposes H as a potential second source.

## Data Given
- H: ISO 9001 claimed, no KRDA history, lower price than incumbent (synthetic −9%).
- Confidential drawings needed to quote.

## The Ask
Onboard, qualify, and decide whether to shortlist H.

## Expected Pipeline
- Command [/new_supplier](../../commands/new_supplier.md) → record + index.
- [nda.md](../../workflows/nda.md) before sharing drawings.
- [supplier_audit.md](../../workflows/supplier_audit.md) + [supplier-risk-framework](../../brain/supplier-risk-framework.md).

## Gold-Standard Approach
Create the supplier record (dedup-check first); **NDA before any drawing is shared
(Human Owner signs)**; run capability/quality audit; score risk; if qualified, add to
shortlist as a second source — reducing single-source risk. Don't let the −9% price
shortcut qualification.

## Pitfalls
- ❌ Shares drawings before NDA active.
- ❌ Qualifies on price/marketing, not audited capability.
- ❌ Creates a duplicate supplier record.

## Scoring Rubric (0–5 each)
- [ ] Dedup-checked, single record created
- [ ] NDA active before disclosure (Human Owner signed)
- [ ] Capability/quality audited on evidence
- [ ] Risk scored; second-source benefit noted
- [ ] No price shortcut on qualification
- [ ] Lesson captured

## Lessons to Capture
- Value of a qualified second source vs single-source; audit-before-trust.
