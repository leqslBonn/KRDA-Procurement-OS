# Late delivery — supplier distress  (SIM-032)

> A supplier's deliveries slip and payment-term requests change — possible financial trouble.

## Context
Frame supplier (qualified) misses deliveries, asks for advance payment / shorter terms.
Quality still OK but signals of cash strain.

## Data Given
- OTD dropping; supplier requests prepayment.
- Single-source for two frame parts.

## The Ask
Protect supply continuity.

## Expected Pipeline
- [delivery-recovery-framework](../../brain/delivery-recovery-framework.md), [supplier-risk-framework](../../brain/supplier-risk-framework.md) (financial/continuity).

## Gold-Standard Approach
Treat as **continuity risk** — escalate (L3). Do not unilaterally agree prepayment (financial
commitment = Human Owner). Assess exposure; **start qualifying an alternative** for the
single-source parts; secure near-term supply (buffer/partial). Log risk; monitor closely.

## Pitfalls
- ❌ Agreeing prepayment to keep parts flowing (commits KRDA cash + risk).
- ❌ Not opening an alternative for single-source exposure.

## Scoring Rubric (0–5 each)
- [ ] Classified as continuity risk · [ ] Escalated (L3) · [ ] No unilateral prepayment
- [ ] Alternative initiated · [ ] Risk logged + monitored · [ ] Lesson captured

## Lessons to Capture
- Financial-distress signals; never commit cash terms without Human Owner.
