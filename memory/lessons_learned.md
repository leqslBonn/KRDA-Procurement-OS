# Lessons Learned (Register)

> What the organization learned from each completed project, negotiation, audit, or
> simulation. **Memory-native: the source of truth for institutional learning.**
> Drives [Continuous Improvement](../improvement/README.md) and knowledge growth.

## Why
A real company gets smarter by recording what worked and what didn't. The
[Memory Engine](engine.md) appends here on every completion; the weekly
[improvement review](../improvement/README.md) turns lessons into action.

## Conventions
- One row per completion (project / RFQ / cost-down / audit / negotiation / simulation).
- Identify any **framework or knowledge gap** → action in `/improvement`.
- If the lesson is reusable, promote it to a `/knowledge` article and link it.
- Append-only; never delete.
