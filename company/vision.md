# Vision

> The long-term direction of the KRDA AI Procurement Department.

## Vision Statement

**To be a self-sustaining AI procurement organization that gives KRDA a decisive
advantage in cost, quality, supply security, and speed — operating as a
disciplined department that grows more capable every year.**

> เป็นองค์กรจัดซื้อ AI ที่ยั่งยืน สร้างความได้เปรียบให้ KRDA ด้านต้นทุน คุณภาพ
> ความมั่นคงของซัพพลาย และความเร็ว — ทำงานอย่างมีวินัย และเก่งขึ้นทุกปี

## The Future We Are Building Toward

1. **A full procurement department in software** — every procurement discipline
   covered by a clear, accountable role.
2. **Compounding knowledge** — every sourcing decision, audit, and cost model
   makes the next one faster and better, because knowledge is captured once and
   reused forever.
3. **Decisions backed by truth** — no purchasing decision rests on a guess; every
   number traces to a source.
4. **Humans in command, AI in operation** — AI does the heavy lifting; humans
   hold the commitment authority.
5. **Trusted supply base** — suppliers are developed into reliable partners, not
   just squeezed on price.

## Horizon Markers

| Horizon | What "good" looks like |
|---------|------------------------|
| Near | Foundation live; first knowledge, workflows, and supplier records in use. |
| Mid | Every category has a strategy; cost-down and RFQ run on reusable workflows. |
| Long | Self-improving: analytics drives sourcing; automation removes manual toil. |

## What This Vision Is Not

- Not a chatbot or a one-off tool.
- Not a replacement for human accountability over commitments.
- Not a place where knowledge is duplicated or decisions go unrecorded.

See the [Mission](mission.md) for what we do today to move toward this vision.
