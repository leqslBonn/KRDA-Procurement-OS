# Mission

> What the department does, for whom, and how — the working purpose behind the
> [Vision](vision.md).

## Mission Statement

**We help KRDA buy the right parts and services, from the right suppliers, at the
right cost and quality, on time — by running procurement as a disciplined,
knowledge-driven, auditable system, while keeping every commitment under human
control.**

> เราช่วย KRDA จัดซื้อชิ้นส่วนและบริการที่ถูกต้อง จากผู้ขายที่เหมาะสม ด้วยต้นทุน
> และคุณภาพที่เหมาะสม ตรงเวลา — โดยทำงานจัดซื้ออย่างเป็นระบบ ขับเคลื่อนด้วยความรู้
> ตรวจสอบย้อนได้ และให้ทุกข้อผูกพันอยู่ใต้การควบคุมของมนุษย์

## Who We Serve

| Stakeholder | What we deliver |
|-------------|-----------------|
| R&D / Engineering | Parts that meet spec, qualified and buyable |
| Production | On-time supply, no line stoppage |
| Finance | Validated savings, controlled, traceable spend |
| Quality | Capable, audited, improving suppliers |
| Human Owner / KRDA | Sound recommendations; commitment authority retained |

## How We Deliver (the value chain we cover)

- **Technical Purchasing** — spec-correct, qualified parts
- **Strategic Sourcing** — category strategy and supplier selection
- **Supplier Development** — capability, quality, and audits
- **Cost Reduction** — should-cost, VA/VE, validated savings
- **RFQ Management** — competitive, comparable quotes and fair awards
- **SAP Purchasing** — clean PR/PO and master data
- **Procurement Analytics** — spend visibility and KPIs
- **Procurement Automation** — removing repetitive manual work

## How We Work

- Reuse before create; link knowledge, never copy it.
- Record outcomes as files; keep everything auditable.
- Recommend freely; commit only through the human approval gate.
