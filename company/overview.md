# KRDA — Company & Procurement Profile

> Single source of truth for KRDA identity. Link here; never copy.

## 1. Organization

- **Full name:** Kubota Research & Development Asia (KRDA)
- **Parent:** SIAM KUBOTA Corporation (Kubota subsidiary)
- **Industry / domain:** Agricultural machinery R&D — ASEAN region
- **Headquarters / site:** Pathumthani, Thailand
- **Established:** 2016
- **Geographic scope:** Thailand · Vietnam · Indonesia · Philippines
- **Staff count (total):** ~200
- **Procurement team:** 7 (1 head + 4 engineers + 2 operations)

## 2. Mission

Contribute to the mechanization of agricultural practice throughout the ASEAN
region by developing machinery customized for local farming conditions and crops.

## 3. Products & Programs

| Program | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Tractor implements | Development of implements for ASEAN soil/crop conditions |
| Combine harvester attachments | Attachments and local compatibility work |
| Rice transplanter attachments | Local adaptation and durability verification |
| Excavator attachments | Region-specific attachment development |
| Crop harvesting machinery | Specialized harvesters: rice, sugar cane, cassava, maize |
| Mechanization systems | Integrated systems: soil preparation → harvesting |

**Target crops:** Rice · Sugar cane · Cassava · Maize

## 4. Facilities & Capabilities

- Bench test equipment (simulation & load testing)
- Dedicated test course
- Basic field testing capability

## 5. Procurement Function

- **Mission of procurement at KRDA:** Source parts, materials, and services that
  enable on-time, on-spec delivery of R&D programs; maintain supplier capability
  aligned to ASEAN agricultural machinery requirements.
- **Key systems:** SAP (purchasing) · `TBD` (other systems)
- **Main stakeholders:** R&D / Engineering · Production · Quality · Finance
- **Spend categories (high level):** General procurement (all company needs) + startup/developer vendors (IT, software, R&D services). See [/knowledge/taxonomy/spend-categories.md](../knowledge/taxonomy/spend-categories.md) for detail.
- **Annual spend (range):** `TBD`
